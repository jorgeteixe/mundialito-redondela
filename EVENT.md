# XLVII Mundialito da Xunqueira 2026

Tournament information and details for the 47th edition of the street football tournament in Redondela (Vigo area).

## General Information

The **Mundialito da Xunqueira** is a well-established street futsal tournament held in the municipality of Redondela. For nearly a month, it brings together dozens of local teams and hundreds of spectators at the town's iconic A Xunqueira court.

- **Organizers:** Concello de Redondela's Sports Department in collaboration with **Club Redondela FS**.
- **Edition:** XLVII (47th Edition).
- **Location:** Pista de A Xunqueira, Redondela.

---

## Key Dates

- **Registration Deadline:** Until **June 23, 2026**.
- **Tournament Start:** Monday, **June 29, 2026**.
- **Tournament End:** Friday, **July 24, 2026**.

---

## Categories and Registration Fees

Registrations are done online on a first-come, first-served basis (limited spots). Each team must have a **minimum of 5 players** and a **maximum of 15**.

### 1. Senior Category

- **Team Limit:** Up to 24 teams.
- **Costs:**
  - €100 registration fee per team.
  - €20 per player (for mandatory medical insurance).

### 2. Cadet Category (Born in 2010 and 2011)

- **Team Limit:** Up to 20 teams.
- **Costs:**
  - €0 registration fee per team (free).
  - €20 per player (for mandatory medical insurance).

---

## Tournament Prizes

### Senior Category

- **1st Place:** €500 cash prize + Official trophy.
- **2nd Place:** €300 cash prize + Official trophy.
- **3rd Place:** Official tournament trophy.

### Cadet Category

- **1st, 2nd, and 3rd Place:** Commemorative trophies from the organization.
